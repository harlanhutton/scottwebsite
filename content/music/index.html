During my fellowships at the Rhodes Institute for Regional Studies and Mike Curb Institute for Music, I founded Rhodes' first record label, New Arboretum Records. Memphis Magazine featured an article about New Arboretum and Rhodes College wrote a cover story.

Rhodes College Cover Story

I am also a professional jazz and rock drummer. I was first chair drummer in the Rhodes College jazz band. I played in a jazz quartet that played events at Rhodes and in the greater Memphis community. I've even opened for Sun Ra Arkestra!
